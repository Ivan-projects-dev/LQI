#Math 
**Coordinate system of space** is system $O, \vec{e}_1, \vec{e}_2, \dots$  
where $O$ is arbitrary fixed point & $\vec{e}_1, \vec{e}_2, \dots$ is basis in this space.  
$O$ - **center**, lines through $O$ & parallel to $\vec{e}_1, \vec{e}_2, \dots$ are **axes**
**Coordinate system** with orthonormal basis is **Cartesian coordinate system**  
If no requirements are imposed on basis - system is **affine**
$\forall M$ any $\vec{OM}$ is **radius [[Vector]]**
