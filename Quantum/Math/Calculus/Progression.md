#Math  
**Arithmetic progression**
$d = a_{n+1} - a_n$
$a_n = a_1 + d(n - 1)$
$a_k = \dfrac{a_{k - 1} + a_{k + 1}}{2}$
$S_n = \dfrac{a_1 + a_n}{2} \cdot n = \dfrac{2a_1 + d(n - 1)}{2} \cdot n$
**Geometric progression**
$b_n = b_1 q^{n - 1}$
$S_n = b_1 \dfrac{q^n - 1}{q - 1}$, for $q \ne 1$
$b_k^2 = b_{k - 1} \cdot b_{k + 1}$    
**Infinite geometric sequence**: $S = \dfrac{b_1}{1 - q}$, for $|q| < 1$
