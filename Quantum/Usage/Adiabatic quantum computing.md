#Quantum #Physics 
[[Quantum adiabatic theorem]] provides us alternative way for quantum computation.

Suppose that $H_f$ is Hamiltonian, & we are interested in learning its ground state. If we init the system at $t=0$ with the ground state of some Hamiltonian whose ground state we already know & that is easy to prepare, the theorem states that the system is likely to end up in the ground state of $H_f$.

It is proven that this model of computation, known as **adiabatic quantum computing**, is equivalent to the standard gate-based model of quantum computation, though this may seem bit counterintuitive at the beginning.
