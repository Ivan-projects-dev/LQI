#Math 
 Tensor is a **N-dimensional [[Matrix]]**:
- **Scalar** is a $0D$ tensor
- **[[Vector]]** is a $1D$ tensor
- **[[Matrix]]** is a $2D$ tensor
![[Pasted image 20251007210335.png]]
Num of directions a tensor can have in a **N**-dimensional space, is called the **Rank** $(R)$ of the tensor.
**Scalar** is a single num.
- It has 0 Axes
- It has a **Rank of 0**
- It is a $0D$ Tensor
**[[Vector]]** is an array of nums.
- It has 1 Axis
- It has a **Rank of 1**
- It is a $1D$ Tensor
**[[Matrix]]** is a 2-dimensional array.
- It has 2 Axis
- It has a **Rank of 2**
- It is a $2D$ Tensor
