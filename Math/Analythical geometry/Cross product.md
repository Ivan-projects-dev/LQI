#Math 
**Cross product identities:**  
$\vec{e}_1 \times \vec{e}_2 = \vec{e}_3$  
$\vec{e}_2 \times \vec{e}_3 = \vec{e}_1$  
$\vec{e}_3 \times \vec{e}_1 = \vec{e}_2$

$(\vec{a} + \vec{b}) \times \vec{c} = \vec{a} \times \vec{c} + \vec{b} \times \vec{c}$
$\vec{a} \times \vec{b} = (a_1 \vec{e}_1 + a_2 \vec{e}_2 + a_3 \vec{e}_3) \times (b_1 \vec{e}_1 + b_2 \vec{e}_2 + b_3 \vec{e}_3)$
Result:  $(a_y b_z - a_z b_y)\vec{e}_1 - (a_x b_z - a_z b_x)\vec{e}_2 + (a_x b_y - a_y b_x)\vec{e}_3$

**Determinant form:**  $$\vec{a} \times \vec{b} = \begin{bmatrix} \vec{e_x} & \vec{e_y} & \vec{e_z} \\a_x & a_y & a_z \\b_x & b_y & b_z\end{bmatrix} $$
