#ML #Math #DB 
**Census** is when we collect data for every member of a group.
**Sample** is when we collect data for some members of a group (less accurate).
**Population** is group of individuals (objects) we want to collect info from.
**Random Sample**: every member of the population has an equal chance to appear there.
**Sampling Bias/Error** occurs when samples are collected in such a way that some individuals are < (or $>$) likely to be included in the sample.
