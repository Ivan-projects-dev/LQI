#Math  
$\lim\limits_{x \to c} a^x = a^c$, $a > 1$    
$\lim\limits_{x \to \infty} a^x = \infty$    
$\lim\limits_{x \to \infty} a^{-x} = 0$
$\lim\limits_{t \to c} \sin t = \sin c$    
$\lim\limits_{t \to c} \cos t = \cos c$
$\lim\limits_{t \to c} \tan t = \tan c$    
$\lim\limits_{t \to c} \cot t = \cot c$
$\lim\limits_{t \to 0} \dfrac{\sin t}{t} = 1$
$\lim\limits_{t \to 0} \dfrac{1 - \cos t}{t} = 0$
$\lim\limits_{x \to c} f(x) = k$
$\lim\limits_{x \to c} [f(x) + g(x)] = \lim f(x) + \lim g(x)$    
$\lim\limits_{x \to c} f(x)g(x) = \lim f(x) \cdot \lim g(x)$
$\lim\limits_{x \to c} \dfrac{f(x)}{g(x)} = \dfrac{\lim f(x)}{\lim g(x)}$    
$\lim\limits_{x \to c} (f(x))^n = (\lim f(x))^n$
