#Math 
**2D [[Cross product]]:**  
$\vec{a} \times \vec{b} = -\vec{b} \times \vec{a}$  
$\vec{a} \times \vec{b} = 0 \iff \vec{a} \parallel \vec{b}$  
$\dfrac{|\vec{a} \times \vec{b}|}{2}$ = **triangle area**  
$|\vec{a} \times \vec{b}|$ = **parallelogram area**
- $\vec{a} \times \vec{b} > 0$ if $(\vec{a}, \vec{b})$ is **right-handed basis**
- $\vec{a} \times \vec{b} < 0$ if $(\vec{a}, \vec{b})$ is **left-handed basis**
$\vec{a} \times \vec{b} = \begin{vmatrix}a_x & a_y \\b_x & b_y\end{vmatrix}$
**Consider 2D curve:**   Let $M$ - **random point**, $F$ - **fixed point**, $d$ - **fixed line outside the curve**, $F$ - **focus**, $d$ - **directrix**  
**Focal param** = **distance between $F$ & $d$**
In [[Coordinate system]] where $F$ is **origin (center)** & directrix $d$ is **orthogonal to $x$-axis** & **resides on the left of $x$-axis**:  
$MK = |x + q|$  
$MF = \sqrt{x^2 + y^2}$
