**High-Performance Computing (HPC)** - use of clusters of processors that work in parallel, to solve complex computational problems that require large amounts of processing power & memory. The key components of an HPC system include the compute nodes, which are typically composed of multi-core processors, high-speed interconnects, & large amounts of memory. The compute nodes are usually connected to shared storage system, which provides access to large datasets & apps. HPC systems also often include specialized hardware accelerators, such as GPUs or FPGAs, which can be used to accelerate specific types of computations.
