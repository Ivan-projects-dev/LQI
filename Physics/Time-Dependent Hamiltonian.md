Hamiltonian may change in time & vary according to some params. In this case, the system evolves according to Schrödinger’s equation with time-dependent Hamiltonian: $H(t)|ψ(t)⟩=iℏddt|ψ(t)⟩$.
Evolution of the system is much $>$ complicated with time-dependent Hamiltonian. In this case, the corresponding unitary transformation is given by $U(t, t_0)=exp(−iℏ∫tt0dt′H(t′))$.

[[Quantum adiabatic theorem]] explains the behaviour of the system when the Hamiltonian changes sufficiently slowly.
